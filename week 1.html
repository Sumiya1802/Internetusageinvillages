WEEKLY REPORT

WEEK- 1 (From 15-05-24 to 19-05-24)

Objective of the Activity Done: SURVEY ON THE RANGE OF AWARENESS OF INTERNET USAGE

Detailed Report:

On day 1:

  • I performed a survey on the awareness of internet usage. I selected an area near my place of living and started the survey.

On day 2:

  • I continued the survey on the second day and focused on the following:
    • The awareness in various places with less internet usage.
    • Questioned about the government plans to increase internet connections.
    • Questioned about the various measures taken by people to maintain high-speed internet connections.

On day 3:

  • Internet usage in the village appears to be limited, with only a few connections.
  • The younger generation seems to be more active online, primarily using social media.

On day 4:

  • Held a community meeting with village people to discuss the usage of the internet.
  • Encouraged residents to share their experiences and concerns regarding internet access.

On day 5:

  • Organized workshops on basic internet skills for residents who are less familiar with technology and received positive feedback from village people.
