WEEKLY REPORT

WEEK- 6 (From 17-06-24 to 22-06-24)

Objective of the Activity Done: SURVEY ON THE RANGE OF AWARENESS OF INTERNET USAGE IN VILLAGE

Detailed Report:

On day 1: Introduction and Setup

  • Orientation session with village leaders and residents.
  • Installation of Wi-Fi hotspots and computer equipment in the community center.
  • Introduction to basic computer operations and internet usage.

On Day 2: Basic Digital Literacy Training

  • Training sessions on how to use a computer, navigate the internet, and create email accounts.
  • Hands-on practice with guidance from trainers.
  • Introduction to internet safety and responsible use.

On day 3: Educational Resources and E-Learning Platforms

  • Demonstrations of various e-learning platforms (e.g., Khan Academy, Coursera).
  • Guidance on how to access and use online courses and educational materials.
  • Setting up user accounts on selected e-learning platforms.

On day 4: Practical Applications and Skill Development

  • Workshops on specific skills (e.g., digital marketing, online farming techniques).
  • Real-life examples of how the internet can improve daily tasks and professional skills.
  • Interactive Q&A sessions to address residents' queries.

On day 5: Collaborative Projects and Peer Learning

  • Group projects utilizing online tools (e.g., creating a community blog, online marketplace).
  • Peer teaching sessions where more knowledgeable residents assist others.
  • Sharing success stories and experiences from the past days.

On day 6: Evaluation and Future Planning

  • Feedback collection from participants about the training sessions and overall experience.
  • Evaluation of the skills learned and areas needing improvement.
  • Discussion on maintaining internet access and continuing education programs.
  • Setting up a committee to oversee ongoing digital education efforts.
