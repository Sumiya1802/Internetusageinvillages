WEEKLY REPORT

WEEK- 5 (From 10-06-24 to 15-06-24)

Objective of the Activity Done: SURVEY ON THE RANGE OF AWARENESS OF INTERNET USAGE IN VILLAGE

Detailed Report:

On day 1: Access to Medical Information

  • Obtain reliable information about diseases, symptoms, treatments, and preventive measures.
  • Access educational content about nutrition, hygiene, and healthy lifestyles.
  • Stay informed about common health issues and how to address them effectively.

On Day 2: Telemedicine and Remote Consultations

  • Connect with doctors and specialists without traveling long distances.
  • Schedule virtual appointments for medical consultations and follow-ups.
  • Receive prescriptions and medical advice online, reducing the need for in-person visits.

On day 3: Health Monitoring and Management

  • Use apps and devices to monitor vital signs such as blood pressure, glucose levels, and heart rate.
  • Keep digital records of medical history, appointments, and treatments.
  • Set reminders for medication and health check-ups.

On day 4: Access to Health Services and Resources

  • Find information about nearby hospitals, clinics, and healthcare providers.
  • Apply for health insurance schemes and government healthcare programs online.
  • Access mental health resources and counseling services through online platforms.

On day 5: Community Support and Awareness

  • Join online support groups for patients with similar health conditions.
  • Share experiences and seek advice from peers and health professionals.
  • Participate in health awareness campaigns and educational webinars.

On day 6: Utilizing Resources

  • Join online communities and forums dedicated to farming and agriculture.
  • Use weather apps for timely updates and farming decisions.
